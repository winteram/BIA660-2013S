Folder IMDB:
ids.txt : the list of movie ids
IMDB.py : IMDB crawler
Sentimental.py: Sentimental analysis using positive.txt and negative.txt files
gross.py : crawler of the box office on IMDB
rotten_tomatoes.py : rotten tomato crawler	

Folder imdb_id and youtube crawler:
imdb_id_and_youtube.py: crawlers generate imdb film ids, youtube trailer views, youtube likes and dislikes number.
ids.txt : the list of movie ids, generated by imdb_id_and_youtube.py
views.txt: films trailer views, likes and dislikes, generated by imdb_id_and_youtube.py
comment.txt : output of the sentimental analysis.
